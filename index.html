System Overview

The Norzagaray College Student Tracking, AI Resolution and Sanction System (NC-STARS) is an exclusive, institutional-grade platform designed specifically for the Administration, Guidance Office, and the Office of Student Affairs.

Recognizing the critical need for a streamlined approach to student discipline and record management, NC-STARS replaces fragmented paperwork with a highly secure, centralized digital vault. It provides real-time tracking of student infractions, seamless endorsement protocols between departments, and an immutable history of all disciplinary actions.

Contextual AI Integration

At the core of the platform is an automated AI Evaluation engine. Upon receiving a case endorsement, the system cross-references the student's behavior against the Norzagaray College Student Handbook (Section M.4) and scans their entire historical record. The AI then objectively recommends appropriate interventions—ranging from basic reprimands to escalated sanctions—ensuring absolute fairness, consistency, and compliance with institutional policies.

Built with enterprise-grade security protocols, including multi-factor authentication and strict role-based access control, NC-STARS guarantees that highly sensitive academic and behavioral data remains strictly confidential and accessible only to authorized college personnel.

NC Student Handbook

Section M.4: Classification of Violation/Offenses & Disciplinary Actions

Disciplinary Action Description
Verbal Warning The student is reprimanded for unacceptable behavior.
Written Reprimand Written statement of the student that a violation of specified policies or regulations has occurred.
Community Service Community service around the school for 15-20 hours but not excluding the student to attend classes.
Suspension Exclusion from classes and privileges. Not less than 5 school days but not more than 20% of the prescribed period.
Dropping/Dismissal Student may be dropped during the school year. Shall be issued transfer credentials immediately.
Expulsion Expelled from Norzagaray College.

Note on Application:

Disciplinary actions shall be imposed commensurate to the degree of gravity of the offense committed. Mitigating or aggravating circumstances shall be considered in determining the appropriate penalty.
