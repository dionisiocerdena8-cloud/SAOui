System Overview

Norzagaray College
Student Tracking, AI Resolution and Sanction System

NC-STARS is the exclusive, institutional-grade platform designed for Norzagaray College. It transforms fragmented paperwork into a highly secure, centralized digital vault for student discipline management.

  • Real-time tracking of student infractions
  • AI-assisted sanction recommendations
  • Immutable history of disciplinary actions

Contextual AI Engine

The system automatically analyzes the student's history and cross-references it against the NC Student Handbook (Section M.4) to provide an objective and fair disciplinary recommendation.

Student Handbook

Section M.4: Classifications & Actions

Disciplinary Action Description
Verbal Warning The student is reprimanded for unacceptable behavior.
Written Reprimand Written statement given to the student regarding a policy violation.
Counseling / Mediation Intervention focused on addressing root causes, retaliation, or conflict resolution.
Community Service Community service around the school for 15-20 hours without affecting class attendance.
Suspension Exclusion from classes and privileges. Not less than 5 school days but not more than 20% of the term.
Dropping/Dismissal The student may be dropped during the school year. Transfer credentials will be issued immediately.
Expulsion Expelled from Norzagaray College.

Application Note

Disciplinary actions shall be imposed commensurate to the degree of gravity of the offense committed. Mitigating or aggravating circumstances shall be considered in determining the appropriate penalty.
